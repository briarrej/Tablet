Chicago: The Black Experience

Chicago is a melting pot for multiple cultures, but the culture of Black people in Chicago is one that is important and extremely personal. There are so many organizations, restaurants, and events ran by Black people that have a positive impact on the community.


Shawn Michelle's

Shawn Michelle’s is a Black-owned Ice Cream parlor. Every dessert at Shawn Michelle’s is homemade, from the ice cream to the pastries. There are over 20 ice cream flavors that drive customers down memory lane. Some of these nostalgic flavors are Lunchroom Butter Cookie, Honey Cinnamon Graham Cracker, Grandma’s Old-Fashioned, and more. They are also known for their amazing cobblers: peach, apple, and blueberry cobblers. Shawn Michelle is located in the Bronzeville community of the South Side of Chicago. During the summer, Shawn Michelle’s lines are usually wrapped around the block! The restaurant truly brings luxury desserts to the community.


Oooh Wee It Is

Oooh Wee It Is is a Black-owned fine dining and takeaway restaurant that has multiple locations throughout the city of Chicago and other cities in Illinois. The location on Chicago’s South Side is their main attraction! The 5000 square foot establishment offers fine dining to the Black community by offering brunch and dinner in an upscale restaurant. Oooh Wee It Is offers multiple soul-food dishes such as macaroni and cheese, candied yams, pot roast, pork chops, baked chicken, and more! The atmosphere of the whole restaurant is welcoming and family-like. This restaurant brings positivity to the community and allows Black Chicagoans to enjoy luxuries in our communities.


Mabe's Deli

Mabe’s Deli is a Black-owned sandwich shop located on Chicago’s South Side. Mabe’s Deli offers healthy and fresh options to choose from on their menu. These items consist of smoothies, such as Orange dreamsicle, Strawberry Banana, Mabe's Green Machine, Smoothie Guru Peanut Butter & Banana, and more. They also offer wraps such as Mabe's Double Decker Turkey Club, Greek Chicken Wrap, DJ's Jerk Turkey Panini, Mae Anna's Jerk Chicken Wrap, and more. My personal favorite is Mae Anna's Jerk Chicken Wrap; it tastes amazing! Mabe’s Deli gives you healthy food with an amazing kick of flavor!
